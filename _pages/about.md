is currently Co-founder and Chief Architect at <span class="text-blue">TARS Robotics (它石智航)</span>. He was selected for the <span class="text-blue">2025 Shanghai Oriental Talent Program – Young Talent Project (东方英才青年计划)</span>, a prestigious municipal talent initiative supporting high-potential young researchers.


He received his Ph.D. from <span class="text-blue">Tsinghua University</span>. Prior to that, he earned his master’s degree from <span class="text-blue">Zhejiang University</span> and his bachelor’s degree from <span class="text-blue">Shanghai Jiao Tong University</span>.

He served as Director of the Intelligent Navigation Department and Chief Expert of Perception at <span class="text-blue">Huawei Autonomous Driving Solution (华为ADS)</span>. There, he led a research team to develop advanced autonomous driving solutions, including <span class="text-blue">Huawei ADS 1.0 and ADS 2.0</span>, which have been widely deployed and commercially applied in iconic mass-produced vehicles such as the AVATR (长安阿维塔) and AITO M5/M7 (问界). He was awarded the <span class="text-blue">2020 Huawei Gold Medal Individual</span>, Huawei’s highest individual honor (top 1%).

After that, he has also held appointments as Chief Scientist of Robotics at <span class="text-blue">Tsinghua University AIR Institute</span>.

His research focuses on <span class="text-blue">artificial intelligence, autonomous driving, robotics, and physical AI</span>.
